About Fruit Maps

This platform connects fruit growers with volunteers at local food pantries. Our vision is to help harvest fruit from people's backyards and bring them to families who don't have access to fresh fruit. Sign up as a fruit grower and list your tree or search your city to see if you can drop by and pick up some fruit on your way to work.

How it works

Post Your Tree

Put your tree on the map when it is ready to harvest!
